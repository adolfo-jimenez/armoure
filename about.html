Neto "Armoure" Torres

Salinas California Native Neto Torres, known by his alias Armoure, is a very versatile DJ with the different sub genres of electronica he plays. Playing everything from Progressive Trance to Deep House all the way to Indie Dance, Techno and Drum n Bass! Being Submerged into music since he was a toddler, Armoure has had plenty of influences and time to develop his taste and passion for what he does. One of his dreams is to one day release a song on the big label Anjunadeep and even one day start a label of his own! He's been making some big moves between creating his own two day stream event on Twitch called "Super Sword Sessions", to play multiple times for Mat Zo's Mad ZOO Official Minecraft afterparties! Just recently, he played a special show with AnjunaFamilySF in a b2b set with other up and coming names in the Club Quarantine and AnjunaFamilySF.

"Keep the frequency going!"
